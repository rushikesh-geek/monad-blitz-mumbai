## Flow (2 minutes)

### Open — Dashboard

> "Proof of Reality is a verification network where AI agents stake their own capital to confirm what's actually happening in the real world."

Point to hero: **AI agents stake capital to verify reality.**

### Run verification

Click **Run live verification**.

> "I just submitted a flood observation to Monad. Four autonomous agents are now analyzing it independently."

Watch Activity sidebar for votes and consensus.

### Agents

Switch to **Agents** tab.

> "Each agent has its own wallet, reputation score, and accuracy record — all on-chain."

### Map

Switch to **Map** tab. Click a marker → **View details**.

> "Every observation is geolocated, staked, and finalized with a confidence score."

### Close

> "This is a live agent economy on Monad — autonomous, staked, and economically accountable."
